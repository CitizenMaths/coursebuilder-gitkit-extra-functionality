Creating and running your account with Citizen Maths
Guidance
Already registered for Citizen Maths? You can sign in to Citizen Maths here.

Starting Citizen Maths for the first time? This is where to create your account with Citizen Maths, before you complete the Citizen Maths registration form.
  • Enter an email address that you use. If this address is one you use for Google services (such as Gmail) you will then be able to log in via Google. If you use any other email address, you will also need to choose a password for your account with Citizen Maths.

Need to reset your Citizen Maths password? You can also do this here by clicking “Problem signing in?” when it appears.

In case of difficulty, email support@citizenmaths.com and we will do our best to assist.
